Production Excellence

From Raw Cotton to Finished Product

Explore our end-to-end textile manufacturing process. We maintain strict oversight at every stage to ensure consistent, premium quality.

Our Manufacturing Process

Vertical Integration & Oversight

By partnering with vertically integrated facilities in Faisalabad, we control the entire production cycle, ensuring efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and quality.

Raw Cotton Selection

The foundation of premium textiles. We carefully source high-grade Pakistani and imported cotton bales, ensuring optimal staple length, strength, and micronaire values. Only the best raw materials enter our spinning mills.

01
🌿

Spinning

Raw cotton is transformed into consistent, high-strength yarn using state-of-the-art ring spinning and open-end machinery. We monitor yarn count, twist, and evenness closely to ensure a flawless final weave.

02

Weaving & Knitting

Yarn is woven or knitted into fabric using advanced air-jet and water-jet looms. We produce a wide range of constructions including percale, sateen, dobby, jacquard, and terry toweling with precise tension control.

03
⚙️

Dyeing & Finishing

Greige fabric undergoes singeing, desizing, bleaching, mercerizing, and dyeing using eco-friendly, azo-free dyes. Advanced finishing processes enhance softness, add anti-microbial properties, or apply stain-resistant treatments.

04
🎨

Cutting

Finished fabric is spread and cut using precision automated CNC cutting machines to ensure exact dimensions, minimal wastage, and perfect pattern matching across all units.

05
✂️

Stitching & Sewing

Skilled operators use modern industrial sewing machines to assemble the products. We maintain strict standards for stitches per inch (SPI), seam strength, and neatness, ensuring durable, high-quality finishes.

06
🪡

Quality Control

Every batch undergoes rigorous AQL-based inspection. Our QC team checks for sizing accuracy, color fastness, stitching defects, and passes products through industrial needle detectors before approval.

07

Packing

Approved products are carefully folded and packed according to your exact specifications. This includes inserting stiffeners, attaching tags, bagging, and placing into durable export cartons.

08
📦

Container Loading

Cartons are systematically loaded into shipping containers under supervision. We optimize space utilization while ensuring the cargo is secure and protected against moisture during transit.

09
🚪

Shipping

Containers are sealed and transported to port. We handle all export documentation, customs clearance, and coordinate with shipping lines to ensure timely delivery to your destination worldwide.
