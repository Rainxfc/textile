End-to-End Textile Services

Our Services

Everything You Need in One Partner

We provide comprehensive textile solutions — eliminating the complexity of international sourcing and giving you peace of mind at every stage.

🔍

Sourcing

We identify and vet the best manufacturing partners in Pakistan for your specific product requirements, ensuring competitive pricing without compromising quality.

🏭

Manufacturing

Full production management across our network of 15+ partner factories. We handle production planning, scheduling, and factory liaison on your behalf.

🔬

Quality Inspection

AQL-based multi-stage inspection at pre-production, inline, and final stages. Third-party lab testing available for international compliance.

✏️

Product Development

From design brief to production-ready samples. We work with your team to develop new products, improve existing lines, and bring your concepts to life.

📦

Packaging

Custom packaging solutions including polybags, box sets, retail packaging, gift wrap, and e-commerce ready packing. Full design and print coordination.

🏷️

Labeling & Branding

Woven labels, printed labels, swing tags, hang tags, and care instruction labels. Full brand identity integration across all products and packaging.

🚢

Container Loading

Supervised container loading with quantity verification, packing inspection, container sealing, and photo documentation for your records.

📋

Export Documentation

Complete export document preparation: commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, certificate of origin, GSP form, and inspection certificates.

🌍

Worldwide Shipping

FCL and LCL sea freight, air freight, and courier solutions to any destination worldwide. We coordinate with trusted freight forwarders for the best rates.

🤝

Vendor Management

Ongoing supplier relationship management, capacity planning, and performance monitoring. We ensure your supply chain remains reliable and responsive.

Our Process

How We Work With You

01

Initial Inquiry

Contact us with your product requirements — quantities, specifications, quality standards, and target pricing. We respond within 24 hours.

02

Quotation & Sampling

We source from our manufacturing network and send you a competitive quotation. Physical samples are dispatched for your approval.

03

Production & QC

Upon order confirmation, production begins under our supervision. Our QC team conducts multi-stage inspections throughout manufacturing.

04

Shipping & Delivery

Verified goods are loaded and shipped with complete export documentation. We track your shipment and keep you informed until delivery.
