Logistics & Shipping

Seamless Door-to-Door Delivery

We take the complexity out of international trade by offering complete logistics solutions, from factory floor to your warehouse.

🚢

Sea Freight (FCL & LCL)

Cost-effective shipping for bulk orders. We load Full Container Loads (FCL) directly at the factory, or manage Less than Container Loads (LCL) through consolidated shipments from Karachi port.

  • 20ft & 40ft HQ Containers
  • Competitive freight rates
  • Secure loading and sealing
✈️

Air Freight & Courier

Rapid delivery for urgent orders, seasonal collections, or physical samples. We partner with leading international couriers (DHL, FedEx) and air freight forwarders via Lahore or Islamabad airports.

  • 3-5 day delivery for samples
  • 7-10 day commercial air freight
  • Real-time tracking
📋

Export Documentation

Our experienced export department handles all necessary paperwork to ensure smooth customs clearance at your destination port, minimizing delays and avoiding demurrage charges.

  • Bill of Lading / Airway Bill
  • Certificate of Origin
  • GSP Form A (for EU/UK duty benefits)

Get a CIF or DDP Quote

We can quote prices inclusive of shipping to your destination port (CIF) or directly to your door (DDP).
