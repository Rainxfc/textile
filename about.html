Our Location Advantage

Why Pakistan? Why Faisalabad?

Pakistan's textile industry is one of the largest in the world. Here's why smart global buyers choose to source from here.

🏭

World's 4th Largest Cotton Producer

Pakistan produces over 8 million bales of cotton annually, giving manufacturers access to high-quality raw materials at competitive costs.

💰

$19+ Billion Annual Textile Exports

Pakistan is one of the world's top 10 textile exporters, with established supply chains and deep manufacturing expertise.

🏙️

Faisalabad — The Manchester of the East

Over 60% of Pakistan's textile production is concentrated in Faisalabad, with thousands of specialized factories covering every category.

🤝

GSP+ Trade Preferences

Pakistan benefits from EU GSP+ status, providing duty-free access to European markets and making Pakistani textiles highly competitive.

⚙️

Vertically Integrated Manufacturing

Pakistan's textile sector covers the full supply chain — from cotton growing and spinning to weaving, dyeing, stitching, and finishing.

🌍

Established Export Infrastructure

Well-connected ports (Karachi, Qasim), bonded warehouses, experienced freight forwarders, and streamlined export documentation.
